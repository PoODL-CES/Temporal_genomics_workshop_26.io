Temporal Population Genomics

A short workshop on laboratory and analysis methods

9th – 12th May, 2026

Raspuri, NCBS, TIFR

About the Workshop

Ancient and historical DNA studies are redefining our understanding of the past. These studies are very exciting, but require very careful labwork and analyses. For the first time in India, we present an opportunity to understand more. Are these approaches right for your question? Can you implement them? Come meet international experts in ancient DNA research and find out.
