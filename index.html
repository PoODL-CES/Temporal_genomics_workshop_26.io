Temporal Genomics Workshop

Ancient DNA • Evolution • Bioinformatics

May 9 – 12, 2026

NCBS, Bangalore

This hands-on workshop introduces participants to temporal population genomics, focusing on ancient DNA analysis, sequencing workflows, and reproducible bioinformatics pipelines.

About the Workshop

Ancient DNA studies are transforming our understanding of evolution and population history. This workshop provides hands-on training in analyzing temporal genomic data using modern tools.
